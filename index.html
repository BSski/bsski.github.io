SERG: web preview

Control the environment to complete achievements and experience the dynamics of an alive ecosystem!
Choose an achievement and fulfill its condition to get a star.

Buttons:

  • Space: Toggle pause
  • ↑↓: Cycle through achievements
  • R: Reset simulation (new terrain & animals)
  • T: Skip 8 days
  • 123: Change right panel option
